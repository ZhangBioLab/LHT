# LHT
This repository is used for processing and analyzing a bioinformatics study on selenium metabolism, selenoproteins, and their evolutionary patterns in bacteria. 
It includes exploring the occurrence of different selenium metabolic pathways in bacteria, providing the most detailed understanding of bacterial selenium utilization to date; 
predicting and identifying new bacterial selenium metabolic pathways and related genes; 
and characterizing all known selenoprotein families and successfully identifying many new selenoprotein families using our previously developed high-performance bacterial selenoprotein gene prediction algorithm, deep-Seq.
